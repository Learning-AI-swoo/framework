Institutional Policy Guide

Recommendations for how TCM professional associations can foster a responsible AI ecosystem.

Reference the AI Competency Framework as a foundational standard for responsible AI use in professional guidelines and codes of ethics.

Develop clear policies ensuring that AI applications used by members meet transparency, fairness, accountability, and risk mitigation expectations.

Promote member education on AI fundamentals, ethical considerations, and the skills needed for critical evaluation of AI tools.

Establish clear reporting mechanisms for practitioners to share AI-related concerns, adverse events, or positive outcomes to inform the community.

Engage with AI developers and researchers to advocate for the creation of inclusive, explainable, and empirically validated tools that respect TCM principles.

This conceptual page was developed by the Education & Training subgroup of the NCCAOM AI Taskforce to illustrate the competency framework.
