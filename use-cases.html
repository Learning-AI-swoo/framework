AI Use Case Scenarios

These examples are for awareness and critical thinking, not as a guide for specific tools. Practitioner judgment is paramount.

Appropriate Use

  • Using an AI-powered language translation tool to assist patient communication during consultations, while ensuring the patient understands it's an assistive technology.
  • Employing an AI system for literature review or treatment planning support, with practitioner oversight and final clinical judgment.

Requires Caution

  • Using AI-generated acupoint recommendations without verifying alignment with patient presentation and AHM pattern differentiation.
  • Relying on AI-generated herbal formula suggestions without thorough practitioner evaluation of herb-drug interactions, contraindications, and individual patient needs.

Inappropriate Use

  • Allowing an AI system to make independent treatment decisions without human validation.
  • Using AI for pulse diagnosis or tongue analysis without direct practitioner interpretation and physical confirmation.
  • Failing to inform patients that AI technologies are involved in their care or data processing.
