AI in AHM: A Framework for Responsible Practice

Guiding the Future of AHM with AI.

To harness the potential of AI while upholding the core values of our profession, this framework provides the essential competencies for practitioners of Acupuncture and Herbal Medicine to engage with AI technologies ethically, responsibly, and effectively.

Six Core Domains

Six pillars for responsible AI engagement in Acupuncture and Herbal Medicine.

AI Literacy & Fundamentals

  • ✓ Explain core AI concepts such as training data, model limits, hallucinations, and inference.
  • ✓ Identify relevant use cases for AI in clinical or administrative workflows.
  • ✓ Review AI-generated information for accuracy and relevance before use.

Transparency & Explainability

  • ✓ Disclose AI use in patient care or administration within appropriate documentation.
  • ✓ Communicate to patients, in plain language, when and how AI informs their care.
  • ✓ Select AI tools that provide understandable and explainable outputs where possible.

Fairness & Inclusivity

  • ✓ Evaluate AI tools for potential biases that could affect different populations.
  • ✓ Advocate for representative, diverse datasets in AI development.
  • ✓ Flag AI outputs that may unintentionally exclude or disadvantage vulnerable groups.

Accountability & Responsibility

  • ✓ Maintain professional accountability for clinical and operational decisions.
  • ✓ Differentiate clearly between AIâ€‘generated suggestions and personal clinical judgment.
  • ✓ Document AI use appropriately within patient records or administrative systems.
  • ✓ Identify situations where legal or regulatory guidance is needed.

Risk Awareness & Mitigation

  • ✓ Use basic risk screening or checklists to evaluate AI tools before adoption.
  • ✓ Identify and respond to performance, safety, or privacy concerns.
  • ✓ Recognize that AI does not replace privacy and informed consent requirements.

Interdisciplinary Collaboration

  • ✓ Collaborate with data scientists, technologists, and ethics experts during AI evaluation.
  • ✓ Help build or serve on AI oversight committees.
  • ✓ Escalate technical or ethical issues promptly to the appropriate stakeholder.
