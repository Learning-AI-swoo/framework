Practitioner Self-Assessment Checklist

Before adopting or using an AI tool in your AHM practice, consider the following questions to ensure responsible engagement.

Transparency

  • Have I clearly disclosed to patients or colleagues that AI is being used?
  • Can I explain in plain language how this AI tool supports, but does not replace, my clinical judgment?

Fairness

  • Have I considered whether this AI tool was developed using representative, inclusive data?
  • Am I aware of potential biases that may affect certain patient groups?

Accountability

  • Am I prepared to take full responsibility for decisions made with AI support?
  • Have I documented AI involvement appropriately in patient records or administrative processes?
  • Am I compliant with all applicable legal and professional regulations when using this tool?

Risk Awareness

  • Have I assessed the reliability, accuracy, and limitations of this AI tool?
  • Do I have a plan to monitor for safety concerns or errors during use?

AI Literacy

  • Do I understand the basic principles behind this AI system (e.g., machine learning, data training)?
  • Am I able to critically evaluate AI outputs before incorporating them into care decisions?
